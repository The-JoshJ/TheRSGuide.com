While the Prayer skill does include utility and skilling effects, it is predominantly a core part of combat.

Prayer points are the **currency** for using prayers. When turning prayers on, your prayer points will drain over time. You can replenish your prayer points by drinking potions such as a **Prayer potion**, or by praying at one of the many altars in the game world.

As your prayer skill level increases, you will gain access to new, more powerful prayers - and increase your maximum prayer points.

## Prayer Books

There are **two prayer books**: the standard prayer book and the ancient curses book.

The standard prayer book is unlocked automatically, while the ancient curses book is unlocked by completing **The Temple of Senntisten** quest. If you want a full walkthrough for this unlock, see the [Ancient Curses guide](/guides/mid-game/ancient-curses). Additional **Seren** curses can be unlocked and added to this book after completion of **The Light Within** quest.

### Protection Prayers

Protection prayers **reduce** damage taken from each style by **50%**, but only one can be activated at once. In OSRS these fully block damage, but in RuneScape it is only a 50% reduction.

There are certain buffs and items such as the **Powder of Protection**, and the **Amulet of Souls** that will increase the effectiveness of your protection prayers beyond 50%. In most cases these do not stack and will max out at a total reduction of 60%.

### Soul Split

At a much higher prayer level, exists Soul Split. This is one of the most powerful prayers in the game, as it heals you for **10%** of the damage dealt, although this does have diminishing returns the higher the damage dealt.

This prayer cannot be used at the same time as a deflect prayer.

### Level 95 Curses

Our next 4 prayers are the most commonly used **damage boosting** prayers, with a prayer for each style. Each of these prayers increases your **accuracy, damage and defence**. The accuracy increase is **+10** levels, the damage increase is **+10%** and the defence increase is **+10** levels.

These prayers also include a **stat drain** on your target, primarily used to drain their defence level for slightly higher accuracy, however some bosses are immune to this.
