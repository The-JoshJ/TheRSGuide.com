## Ability Settings

### Allow Ability Queueing

<SplitContent>
  <SplitItem width={85}>

This setting is personal preference. When toggled on, it will queue a pressed ability until it goes off. When toggled off, you will need to press the ability the tick before wanting to cast it.

If you spam keybinds, it is recommended to keep ability queueing off. The majority of players don't use ability queueing.

  </SplitItem>
  <SplitItem width={15}>
    ![Ability Queueing Icon](https://www.thersguide.com/1756997387461-Screenshot_2025-09-03_at_2.00.56_am.jpg)
  </SplitItem>
</SplitContent>

### Automatically Trigger Regenerate

Regenerate consumes your remaining adrenaline and converts it into lifepoints. It can only be used out of combat.

**Recommendation:** Keep this **disabled**. If you want to use Regenerate, activate it manually from the Constitution book or by clicking the heart icon next to your lifepoints.

## Action Bar Management

### Display Additional Action Bars

You start with 1 primary action bar but can display up to 4 additional bars on screen. Add these as needed for more keybind space.

### Change Action Bar Preset Names

Name your bars so you can easily identify them when switching presets or adding additional bars to your interface.
