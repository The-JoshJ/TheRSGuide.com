## Combat Triangle

The combat triangle is the baseline relationship between the three original combat styles:

- **Ranged** is strong against **Magic**
- **Magic** is strong against **Melee**
- **Melee** is strong against **Ranged**

If you are not targeting a specific monster weakness, following the combat triangle is a good default way to improve your hit chance.

## Weakness

Weakness is one of the easiest ways to increase your damage output. This can be achieved by using a specific class like **Ranged** or **Melee**, or a specific weakness like **Slash Weapons** or **Earth Spells**.

Specific elemental weaknesses sit on top of the combat triangle. For example, if a monster is weak to **Earth Spells**, it will also generally be weak to **Magic** as a style, but **Earth Spells** will be an even better option.

Because the maximum damage potential you can reach is 100%, if you are already at 100% hit chance, using the target's weakness will have **no** impact on your damage.
