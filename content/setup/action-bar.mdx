The action bar is your primary combat interface in RuneScape. It holds your abilities, displays vital stats, and gives you quick access to essential combat functions.

![Empty Action Bar](https://runescape.wiki/images/Empty_action_bar.png)

---

## Status Icons

The left side of the action bar displays four important status indicators. Understanding these is essential for effective combat.

### Lifepoints

<SplitContent>
  <SplitItem width={15}>
    <Center>
      <img src="https://www.thersguide.com/images/gui/health.png" alt="Lifepoints" className="w-8 h-8" />
    </Center>
  </SplitItem>
  <SplitItem width={85}>
    Shows your current and maximum health. When this reaches zero, you die. Food and certain abilities can restore lifepoints during combat. Keep an eye on this at all times.
  </SplitItem>
</SplitContent>

### Adrenaline

<SplitContent>
  <SplitItem width={15}>
    <Center>
      <img src="https://www.thersguide.com/images/gui/adrenalinev2.png" alt="Adrenaline" className="w-8 h-8" />
    </Center>
  </SplitItem>
  <SplitItem width={85}>
    Adrenaline is the resource you build and spend in combat. Basic abilities generate adrenaline, while powerful threshold and ultimate abilities consume it. Ultimate abilities require 100% adrenaline to activate.

    **Tip:** Clicking this icon toggles **Auto-Retaliate**, which makes your character automatically fight back when attacked.
  </SplitItem>
</SplitContent>

### Prayer Points

<SplitContent>
  <SplitItem width={15}>
    <Center>
      <img src="https://www.thersguide.com/images/gui/prayer-points.png" alt="Prayer Points" className="w-8 h-8" />
    </Center>
  </SplitItem>
  <SplitItem width={85}>
    Displays your current and maximum prayer points. Prayers drain these points while active, providing powerful combat bonuses.

    **Tip:** Right-click to set up **Quick Prayers**, then left-click or use a keybind to toggle them all at once.
  </SplitItem>
</SplitContent>

### Summoning Points

<SplitContent>
  <SplitItem width={15}>
    <Center>
      <img src="https://www.thersguide.com/images/gui/summoning-points.png" alt="Summoning Points" className="w-8 h-8" />
    </Center>
  </SplitItem>
  <SplitItem width={85}>
    Shows your current and maximum summoning points, used by your familiar's special abilities.

    **Tip:** Right-click for quick access to special move scrolls, familiar inventory, and the "Call Familiar" option.
  </SplitItem>
</SplitContent>

---

## Ability Slots

The 14 slots in the center of the action bar are where you place your abilities. You can drag abilities from the Powers interface or right-click a slot to customize it.

> For recommended ability setups, see our [Combat Basics](/getting-started/combat-basics) guide.
