Jumping into a new game, let alone a new MMO, can be daunting. Especially one as unique as **RuneScape**. RuneScape is a sandbox MMO about adventure, self-discovery, and most importantly, forging your own path.

## What This Guide Covers

This website has been designed to give you everything you need to help you along the start of your adventure:

- **Settings & Interfaces**: Get your client configured for success
- **Combat Fundamentals**: Learn how abilities, damage, and the tick system work
- **Progression Paths**: Clear goals from early game through endgame content
