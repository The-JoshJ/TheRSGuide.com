Jumping into a new game, let alone a new MMO, can be daunting. **RuneScape** is a sandbox MMO about adventure, self-discovery, and most importantly, forging your own path.

## What This Guide Covers

This website has been designed by experts who know what it's like to be a new player and gives you everything you need to help you at the beginning of your adventure:

- **First Time Settings & User Interface:** Familiarize yourself with the game interface, and get your RuneScape client set up for success.
- **Combat Fundamentals:** Learn the basics of combat in RuneScape; damage, abilities, and the tick system. 
- **Progression Paths and Skill Training:** Everything you need to set account goals, and achieve them.
