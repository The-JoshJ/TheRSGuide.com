## Overview

Enhanced abilities are powerful ranged attacks that replace the old threshold system. These abilities interact with **Searing Winds** and **Shadow Imbued** buffs for increased effectiveness.

## Simple Enhanced

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Snap_Shot.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Snap Shot

Unlocked at level **2**, Snap Shot fires two shots in quick succession dealing **135%-155%** damage per hit with **2 hits**. Damage is **60%** effective in PvP.

## Channelled Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Snipe.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Snipe

Unlocked at level **7**, Snipe fires a precise shot dealing **300%-360%** damage after **1.8s** with a **1 minute** cooldown. This ability is channelled. In PvP, this ability disables the target's **Protection Prayers** and deals **75%** effectiveness.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Rapid_Fire.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Rapid Fire

Unlocked at level **62**, Rapid Fire rapidly fires at the target dealing **75%-85%** damage per hit every **0.6s** with **8 hits** and a **20.4s** cooldown. This ability is channelled but you **can move** while channeling. It **binds** the target for **6s** and each attack **extends the duration of Searing Winds** by **0.6s**. Damage is **80%** effective in PvP.

## Area of Effect

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Bombardment.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Bombardment

Unlocked at level **36**, Bombardment fires a volley of shots into the air dealing **220%-260%** damage to the target and up to **9 additional enemies** within 2 tiles of the target with a **5.4s** cooldown. Damage is **60%** effective in PvP.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Corruption_Shot.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Corruption Shot

> This ability is obtained via a codex unlock at level **70**.

Corruption Shot corrupts the target and nearby enemies, causing them to take damage over time. It deals **90%-110%** damage per hit every **1.2s** with **5 hits** to the target and up to **5 additional enemies** within 5 tiles. Damage is reduced by **20% of initial damage** with each hit. This ability has a **15s** cooldown.

## Damage Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Shadow_Tendrils.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Shadow Tendrils

> This ability is obtained via a quest unlock at level **75**.

Shadow Tendrils whips at your feet before striking the target dealing **200%-240%** damage with a **45s** cooldown. This ability deals **100%-135% damage to yourself** but is **guaranteed to critically strike**. It also extends the duration of **Shadow Imbued** by **3.6s**.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Imbue-_Shadows.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Imbue: Shadows

Unlocked at level **90**, Imbue: Shadows imbues your weapon with dark whispers applying **Shadow Imbued** to yourself for **30s** with a **1 minute** cooldown. This ability costs **40% adrenaline**.

**Shadow Imbued**: Ranged attacks generate **5% adrenaline** with each hit.
