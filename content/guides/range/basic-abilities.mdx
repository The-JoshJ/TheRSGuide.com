## Overview

Ranged has fewer basics than Melee, but more than Necromancy, for a nice middle ground. Ranged and Magic have quite a few **similar basics** that perform the same, so this can help with familiarity. There are also some abilities that perform similar to Melee, but significantly less.

All of these Ranged Basic Abilities will generate a base of **8% adrenaline**.

## Simple Basics

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Dazing_Shot.png?cf0bd" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Dazing Shot

Dazing Shot is a low cooldown, medium damage ability with an average of **105%** damage with a **5.4s** cooldown. When cast this will reduce your target's **Hit Chance** by **10%** for **4.2s**. This ability can only be cast with a **2h** weapon.

## Conditional Damage

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Piercing_Shot.png?6b1f3" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Piercing Shot

Piercing Shot is a low cooldown, low damage ability with an average of **50%** damage per hit, dealing **2** hits for a total average of **100%** and a **3s** cooldown. Damage is increased by **30%** to **130%** if used on a stunned or bound target.

## Channelled Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Snipe.png?cf0bd" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Snipe

This is the only **channelled** Ranged basic, dealing **185%** average damage after charging up for **2.4s**, with a cooldown of **10.2s**. This charge time is **1 tick** longer than the normal global cooldown of **1.8s**.

## Bleeds

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Fragmentation_Shot.png?5e685" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Fragmentation Shot

Fragmentation Shot is a damage over time basic, dealing **28%** average damage per hit, for **5** hits over **5.4s**, for a total of **140%**. If the target moves, you will deal **2x** damage for a total of **280%**. The cooldown is **15s**.

## Area of Effect

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Corruption_Shot.png?ac6e7" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Corruption Shot

> This ability is obtained via a codex unlock.

Despite being in the AoE section, Corruption Shot is importantly **also** a bleed, but it is mainly used for AoE situations.

This will deal a hit to your target of **70%**. Each subsequent hit will be reduced by **20%** of the initial hit for a total of **5 hits**: 70%, 56%, 42%, 28%, 14%, with an overall value of **210%** and a cooldown of **15s**.

Additionally, this will spread to all targets within **2** tiles of the primary target, and deal all remaining hits to all targets. This can spread continuously to other targets, until all 5 hits have occurred.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Ricochet.png?a50f0" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Ricochet

Ricochet deals **70%** average damage to your target, and up to **2** additional targets within **5** tiles of your main target. Ricochet has a cooldown of **10.2s**.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Ricochet.png?93e69" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Ricochet** via an ability codex.
>
> Greater Ricochet works similarly to Ricochet but provides an additional effect. If you do not have any additional targets around you, Ricochet will instead deal the **2** additional hits onto your primary target, dealing **35%** average damage each.

## Stuns

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Binding_Shot.png?5e685" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Binding Shot

Binding Shot deals **70%** average damage, **stunning** the target for **1.2s** and **binding** the target for **9.6s**, with a **15s** cooldown.

## Ability Synergy

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Needle_Strike.png?c028b" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Needle Strike

Needle Strike deals **105%** average damage and has a **5.4s** cooldown. When cast, this will increase the damage of your next ability by **7%**. Needle Strike can only be cast with **dual wield** weapons.
