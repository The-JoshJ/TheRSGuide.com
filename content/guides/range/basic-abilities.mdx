## Overview

Ranged basic abilities focus on building and maintaining **Searing Winds**, a buff that increases your damage output. Many abilities interact with **Snipe** by reducing its cooldown.

All of these Ranged Basic Abilities will generate a base of **9% adrenaline**.

## Core Basics

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Ranged_%28ability%29.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Basic Attack

Basic Attack is your default ability dealing **110%-130%** damage with no cooldown.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Piercing_Shot.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Piercing Shot

Unlocked at level **13**, Piercing Shot fires two piercing shots dealing **45%-55%** damage per hit with **2 hits** and a **3s** cooldown. Each hit reduces the cooldown of **Snipe** by **2.4s**.

## Buff Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Galeshot.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Galeshot

Unlocked at level **58**, Galeshot fires an infused shot at the target dealing **90%-110%** damage with a **20.4s** cooldown. This ability applies **Searing Winds** to yourself for **6s**.

**Searing Winds**: Ranged attacks deal an additional **20% bonus damage** with each hit.

## Area of Effect

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Ricochet.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Ricochet

Unlocked at level **67**, Ricochet fires a shot which ricochets off the target dealing **75%-85%** damage to the target and up to **2 additional enemies** within 5 tiles. If no additional targets can be found, the target will be hit for an additional **15%-20%** damage for each missing enemy. This ability has a **10.2s** cooldown.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Ricochet.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Ricochet** via an ability codex for enhanced multi-target damage.

## Stuns

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Binding_Shot.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Binding Shot

Unlocked at level **31**, Binding Shot fires a binding shot dealing **65%-75%** damage with a **15s** cooldown. This ability **stuns** the target for **1.2s** and **binds** the target for **9.6s**. It holds **2 charges** (second charge unlocks at level **54**).
