## Overview

Ranged Ultimate abilities are your most powerful attacks. These cost adrenaline to use.

## Ultimate Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Deadshot.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Deadshot

Unlocked at level **21**, Deadshot fires an enchanted shot at the target striking them multiple times dealing **105%-125%** damage per hit with **4 hits**. This ability costs **60% adrenaline** with a **30s** cooldown. Damage is **60%** effective in PvP.

> This ability can be upgraded by obtaining the [Igneous Kal-Xil](/guides/late-game/zuk-cape), increasing the number of hits and overall damage output.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Death%27s_Swiftness.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Death's Swiftness

> This ability requires a quest to unlock at level **76**.

Death's Swiftness is an Ultimate that is cast around your location, empowering you with a significant damage boost. While standing inside your Death's Swiftness, all Ranged attacks are increased.

Note that **bleed** abilities **do not** benefit from this buff.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Death%27s_Swiftness.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Death's Swiftness** via an ability codex for increased duration.
