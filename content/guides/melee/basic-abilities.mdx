## Overview

Melee basic abilities generate **Bloodlust stacks** which can be consumed by Enhanced abilities for bonus effects. Your main ultimate, **Berserk**, increases your maximum Bloodlust stacks and makes basic abilities generate stacks faster.

All of these Melee Basic Abilities will generate a base of **9% adrenaline** unless stated otherwise.

## Core Basics

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Attack_%28ability%29.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Basic Attack

Basic Attack is your default ability dealing **110%-130%** damage. It generates **1 Bloodlust stack** and has no cooldown.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Adaptive_Strike.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Adaptive Strike

Unlocked at level **7**, Adaptive Strike channels your weapon's form into an adaptive strike dealing **60%-75%** damage per hit with **2 hits**. This ability has a **5.4s** cooldown and generates **12% adrenaline** instead of the standard 9%.

The ability changes based on your equipped weapon:
- **2h**: Swipe your weapon in front of you
- **Dual Wield**: Strike at the target with both weapons

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Rend.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Rend

Unlocked at level **18**, Rend slices through the target dealing **135%-165%** damage with a **10.2s** cooldown. Notably, this ability generates **2 Bloodlust stacks** instead of the standard 1.

## Conditional Damage

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Punish.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Punish

Unlocked at level **60**, Punish slashes at the target unexpectedly dealing **110%-130%** damage with a **24s** cooldown. This ability will deal **2.5x damage** if the target's Life Points are below **50%**, leading to a potential **275%-325%** damage.

## Stuns

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Backhand.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Backhand

Unlocked at level **31**, Backhand strikes the target dealing **95%-105%** damage and **stunning and binding** the target for **1.8s**. This ability has a **15s** cooldown and holds **2 charges** (second charge unlocks at level **54**).

## Ability Synergy

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Fury.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Fury

Unlocked at level **21**, Fury swings your weapon at the target dealing **110%-130%** damage with a **15s** cooldown. When cast, the **Critical Strike Chance** of your next Melee attack is increased by **25%**.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Fury.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Fury** via an ability codex - increases damage to **120%-140%** and your next Melee attack within **15s** is **guaranteed to Critically Strike**.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Barge.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Barge

Unlocked at level **65**, Barge moves you up to **10 tiles** towards the target dealing **75%-95%** damage with a **20.4s** cooldown. Casting this will **clear any Bound debuff** on yourself and instead **bind your target** for **6.6s**.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Barge.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Barge** via an ability codex.
>
> The movement and bind effects remain the same. The base damage also remains the same, but additional **5%-7%** damage will be dealt for every **0.6s** since your last attack, up to a maximum of **6s**.
>
> Additionally, if you have not attacked for **4.8s**, your next **Channeled ability** cast within **6s** will have its damage dealt as **Damage over Time** instead of requiring channeling.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Chaos_Roar.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Chaos Roar

> This ability is obtained via a codex unlock at level **92**.

Chaos Roar releases a savage war cry dealing **100%-120%** damage with a **60s** cooldown. Your next Melee ability within **7.2s** will deal **1.75x** base damage (reduced to **1.25x** in PvP).
