## Overview

Melee Ultimate abilities are your most powerful attacks. These cost adrenaline to use - either **60%** or **100%** depending on the ability.

---

## Ultimate Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Overpower.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Overpower

Unlocked at level **15**, Overpower strikes the target with a massive overhead swing dealing **550%-600%** damage. This ability costs **60% adrenaline** with a **30s** cooldown. Damage is **55%** effective in PvP.

> This ability can be upgraded by obtaining the [Igneous Kal-Ket](/guides/late-game/zuk-cape), increasing the number of hits and overall damage output.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Pulverise.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Pulverise

Unlocked at level **71**, Pulverise charges up a massive strike dealing **300%-340%** damage and applying **Pulverised** to the target for **30s**. This ability costs **60% adrenaline** with a **1 minute** cooldown and can only be cast with a **2h weapon**.

**Pulverised**: The target deals **25% less damage** while affected.

If your target dies to this ability, you will be granted **50% adrenaline**.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Berserk.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Berserk

Unlocked at level **76**, Berserk empowers yourself for **19.8s** with a **1 minute** cooldown. This ability costs **100% adrenaline**.

During Berserk:
- Melee attacks deal **1.75x damage**
- You take **25% more damage**
- Generates **4 Bloodlust stacks** on cast
- Basic attacks and abilities generate **2x Bloodlust stacks**
- Maximum Bloodlust stacks increased by **4**
- **Overpower** cooldown reduced to **9s**

Note that **bleed** abilities **do not** benefit from this buff. The duration can be extended via certain high level armour.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Meteor_Strike.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Meteor Strike

Unlocked at level **90**, Meteor Strike jumps up and strikes the ground dealing **220%-250%** damage to the target and up to **8 additional enemies** within 1 tile. This ability costs **60% adrenaline** with a **1 minute** cooldown.

For the next **30s**:
- Melee basic abilities generate **1.5x adrenaline**
- Generates **4.5% adrenaline** every **0.6s** while you have a Melee weapon equipped
