## Overview

Enhanced abilities are powerful attacks that can consume **Bloodlust stacks** for additional effects.

---

## Channelled Abilities

All abilities in this section are Channelled Abilities. Players **can** move during melee channelled abilities, as long as they remain within **attack range** of their target.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Assault.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Assault

Unlocked at level **3**, Assault strikes at the target multiple times dealing **145%-175%** damage per hit every **1.2s** with **4 hits** and a **6s** cooldown. This ability is channelled but you can move while channeling. Damage is **75%** effective in PvP.

> **Bloodlust** (consumes 4 stacks): Deals **185%-215%** damage per hit instead.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Flurry.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Flurry

Unlocked at level **45**, Flurry swings both weapons rapidly around you dealing **65%-75%** damage per hit every **0.6s** with **8 hits** to up to **8 enemies** within 1 tile. This ability has a **20.4s** cooldown and will **stun and bind** the target for **3.6s**. Each attack extends the duration of **Berserk** by **0.6s**.

> **Bloodlust** (consumes 4 stacks): Deals **1% increased damage** for each **1% Life Points** the target is missing, up to a maximum of **65%** bonus damage.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Flurry.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Flurry** via an ability codex for increased damage and extending the duration of <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Berserk.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> Berserk by 0.6s for each hit of the attack.

## Area of Effect

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Hurricane.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Hurricane

Unlocked at level **37**, Hurricane spins you on the spot dealing **135%-165%** damage with **2 hits**. The second hit deals **155%-185%** damage to the target and up to **9 additional enemies** within 1 tile of you. The cooldown of Hurricane is reduced by **3s** for each enemy hit. Base cooldown is **20.4s**. Damage is **55%** effective in PvP.

> **Bloodlust** (consumes 4 stacks): Adds a **third hit** dealing **75%-95%** damage to the target and up to 9 additional enemies.

## Bleeds

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Dismember.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Dismember

Unlocked at level **50**, Dismember slices at the target causing them to **Bleed** dealing **25%-35%** damage per hit every **1.2s** with **8 hits**. This ability heals you for **4%** of the damage dealt and has a **24s** cooldown. It can be **recast within 24s** of the previous cast for more powerful follow-up abilities.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Slaughter.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Slaughter (Second Cast)

Unlocked at level **60**, Slaughter is the second cast of Dismember. It stabs the target causing them to **Bleed** dealing **80%-100%** damage per hit every **1.8s** with **6 hits**. Heals you for **8%** of the damage dealt.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Massacre.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Massacre (Third Cast)

Unlocked at level **75**, Massacre is the third cast of Dismember. It swings at the target causing them to **Bleed** dealing **110%-130%** damage on the first hit, then **120%** damage per hit every **2.4s** with **7 hits** total. Heals you for **12%** of the damage dealt.
