In the late game, we'll be focusing on the most critical unlocks in RuneScape. This includes completing the **Extinction** quest for the **Ring of Vigour passive**, unlocking **Amascut, the Devourer** boss fight, creating **Overloads**, and getting your first **Zuk cape**!
