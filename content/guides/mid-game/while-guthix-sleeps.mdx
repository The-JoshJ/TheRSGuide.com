title: While Guthix Sleeps
description: Major quest unlocking Dragon Forge, XP lamps, and Tormented Demons
---

Completing **While Guthix Sleeps** unlocks several major mid-game milestones:

- **Access to the Dragon Forge:** This unique forge is required to create some of the best-in-slot (BiS) Ranged gear, as well as to craft and smith high-level masterwork weapons and equipment. The Dragon Forge is a key progression point for players looking to upgrade their arsenal and maximize their combat effectiveness.
- **400,000 experience lamp:** You will receive a lamp that grants 400,000 experience in any skill of your choice, provided the skill is at least level 65. This is a significant boost for training expensive or slow skills at this stage of the game.
- **Access to Tormented Demons:** These powerful monsters are the exclusive source of **Dragon Claws**, a highly sought-after weapon for both PvM and PvP. Tormented Demons also drop other valuable loot, making them a lucrative and challenging new boss to farm.

Overall, **While Guthix Sleeps** is a pivotal quest for unlocking new content, powerful gear upgrades, and valuable training opportunities.
