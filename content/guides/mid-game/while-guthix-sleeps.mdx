---
title: While Guthix Sleeps
description: Major quest unlocking Dragon Forge, XP lamps, and Tormented Demons
---

Completing **While Guthix Sleeps** unlocks several major mid-game milestones:

- **Access to the Dragon Forge:** This unique forge is required to create some of the best ranged gear in RuneScape as well as masterwork equipment.
- **4 x 100,000 experience lamp:** You will receive 4 lamps that grant 100,000 experience each in any skill of your choice, provided the skill is at least level 65. This is a massive amount of experience for this stage of the game and it can be very powerful for powerleveling more difficult skills like Herblore and Agility.
- **Access to Tormented Demons:** These powerful monsters are the exclusive source of **Dragon Claws**, a useful Special Attack weapon.

Overall, **While Guthix Sleeps** is a pivotal quest for unlocking new content, powerful gear upgrades, and valuable training opportunities.
