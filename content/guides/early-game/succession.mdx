---
title: Succession
description: Unlock the Dive ability and Infernal Puzzle Box passive
---

Completing **Succession** unlocks the ability to place your **Infernal Puzzle Box** on your toolbelt, turning its effect into a passive. It prevents adrenaline from draining outside of combat and is a major quality-of-life upgrade for all combat styles.

You also gain access to the **Dive** ability, a crucial movement ability that allows you to quickly reposition in both PvM and skilling scenarios.
