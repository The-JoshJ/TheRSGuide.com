Completing Desert Treasure grants you access to **Ancient Magicks**, a powerful spellbook that is widely considered the best for magic-based PvM (Player vs Monster) activities.

Ancient Magicks offers a variety of high-damage and area-of-effect spells, making it invaluable for both bossing and slayer tasks. Unlocking this spellbook is a major milestone for any account focused on maximizing magic combat effectiveness.
