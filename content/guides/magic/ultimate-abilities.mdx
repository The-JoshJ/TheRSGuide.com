## Overview

Magic Ultimate abilities are your most powerful attacks. These cost adrenaline to use.

---

## Ultimate Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Omnipower.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Omnipower

Unlocked at level **12**, Omnipower bombards the target with each of the four elements dealing **420%-500%** damage. This ability costs **60% adrenaline** with a **30s** cooldown. Damage is **60%** effective in PvP.

> This ability can be upgraded by obtaining the [Igneous Kal-Mej](/guides/late-game/zuk-cape), increasing the number of hits and overall damage output.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Sunshine.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Sunshine

> This ability requires a quest to unlock at level **76**.

Sunshine is an Ultimate that is cast around your location, empowering you with a significant damage boost. While standing inside your Sunshine, all Magic attacks are increased.

Note that **bleed** abilities **do not** benefit from this buff.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Sunshine.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Sunshine** via an ability codex for increased duration.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Tsunami.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Tsunami

> Unlocked at level **90**.

Tsunami is a powerful area of effect ultimate dealing **225%-275%** damage to your target and up to **8 additional targets** in a frontal area. You will also gain adrenaline from **Critical Strikes** for a duration after casting. Critical Strikes generated from the initial cast will generate adrenaline.
