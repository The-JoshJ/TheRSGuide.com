## Overview

Magic basic abilities focus on the **Flow** mechanic which reduces the adrenaline cost of your next ability. Many abilities also interact with status effects like **Combusted** for bonus damage.

All of these Magic Basic Abilities will generate a base of **9% adrenaline**.

## Core Basics

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Magic_%28ability%29.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Basic Attack

Basic Attack is your default ability dealing **110%-130%** damage with no cooldown.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Sonic_Wave.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Sonic Wave

Unlocked at level **6**, Sonic Wave is a focused blast of magic that pummels the target dealing **90%-110%** damage with a **15s** cooldown. When cast, this applies **Flow** to yourself for **9s**.

**Flow**: Your next Magic ability costs **10% less adrenaline**.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Sonic_Wave.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Sonic Wave** via an ability codex for increased damage and Flow duration.

## Area of Effect

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Dragon_Breath.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Dragon Breath

Unlocked at level **19**, Dragon Breath breathes a mighty cloud of fire dealing **110%-130%** damage to the target and up to **4 additional enemies** in a cone in the attack direction with a **7.2s** cooldown. Deals **1.25x damage** to **Combusted** enemies.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Chain.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Chain

Unlocked at level **51**, Chain fires a magical spell that chains between nearby targets dealing **70%-90%** damage to the target and up to **2 additional enemies** within 5 tiles with a **10.2s** cooldown. Your next Magic ability against the target within **6s** will also target the additional enemies, dealing **30% damage**.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Chain.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Chain** via an ability codex for enhanced multi-target damage.

## Bleeds

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Combust.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Combust

Unlocked at level **38**, Combust explodes the target causing them to **Burn** dealing **27%-33%** damage per hit every **1.8s** with **10 hits** and an **18s** cooldown.

## Stuns

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Impact.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Impact

Unlocked at level **31**, Impact smashes the target with a burst of earth energy dealing **65%-75%** damage with a **15s** cooldown. This ability **stuns and binds** the target for **1.8s** and holds **2 charges** (second charge unlocks at level **54**).

## Channelled Abilities

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Concentrated_Blast.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Concentrated Blast

Unlocked at level **66**, Concentrated Blast blasts the target with beams of concentrated energy attacking **3 times** over **1.8s** dealing **30%-40%** damage per hit with a **5.4s** cooldown. This ability is channelled. Each attack increases the **Critical Strike Chance** of your next Magic attack by **5%**, stacking to a maximum of **15%**.

> Can be upgraded to <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Concentrated_Blast.png" className="inline h-5 w-5" /> **Greater Concentrated Blast** via an ability codex for increased damage and compressed channel time.
