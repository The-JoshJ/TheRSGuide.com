## Overview

Enhanced abilities are powerful magic attacks that replace the old threshold system. These abilities provide high damage output and utility effects.

## Simple Enhanced

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Wild_Magic.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Wild Magic

Unlocked at level **3**, Wild Magic fires an unpredictable double magical attack at the target dealing **125%-155%** damage per hit with **2 hits** and a **5.4s** cooldown.

## Channelled Abilities

All abilities in this section are Channelled Abilities. Players are **unable to move** during magic channelled abilities.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Asphyxiate.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Asphyxiate

Unlocked at level **59**, Asphyxiate reaches out with magical force and chokes the target dealing **110%-130%** damage per hit every **1.2s** with **4 hits** and a **20.4s** cooldown. This ability **stuns and binds** the target for **3.6s**.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Smoke_Tendrils.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Smoke Tendrils

> This ability is obtained via a quest unlock at level **75**.

Smoke Tendrils whips at the target's feet dealing **55%-65%** damage per hit every **1.2s** with **4 hits** and a **45s** cooldown. This ability deals an additional **10%-15% damage** with each hit but also deals **35%-40% damage to yourself** per hit. All hits are **guaranteed to critically strike**.

## Area of Effect

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Corruption_Blast.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Corruption Blast

> This ability is obtained via a codex unlock at level **70**.

Corruption Blast blasts the target with corruption causing them to take damage over time dealing **90%-110%** damage per hit every **1.2s** with **5 hits** and a **15s** cooldown. Damage is reduced by **20% of initial damage** with each hit and will **spread to enemies within 2 tiles**.

### <img src="https://runescape.wiki/images/Magma_Tempest.png" className="inline h-6 w-6" /> Magma Tempest

> This ability is obtained via a codex unlock at level **85**.

Magma Tempest creates a storm of fire and earth around the target creating a **5x5 area** at the target location dealing **35%-45%** damage per hit every **1.2s** with **8 hits** to up to **25 enemies** inside the area with a **21s** cooldown. This ability **cannot critically strike**.

**Note:** There is also a targeted variant that can be manually placed. This must be manually triggered during revolution combat.
